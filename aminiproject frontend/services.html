Our Services

What We Offer

At CarePlus Clinic, we provide a comprehensive range of medical services designed to meet the diverse needs of our patients. From routine check-ups to complex surgical procedures, our dedicated team ensures world-class care.

General Medicine

Diagnosis and treatment of a wide range of common illnesses and chronic conditions.

Cardiology

Expert care for heart-related conditions including diagnostics, treatment, and rehabilitation.

Dermatology

Advanced skin care services including acne, eczema, cosmetic treatments and more.

Neurology

Comprehensive diagnosis and management of disorders of the brain and nervous system.

Orthopedics

Specialized treatment for bone, joint, and musculoskeletal disorders including surgery.

Laboratory & Diagnostics

Accurate lab testing, imaging, and diagnostic support using state-of-the-art technology.

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency department staffed with experienced medical professionals and rapid response.

Pediatrics

Compassionate and expert care for infants, children, and adolescents.
